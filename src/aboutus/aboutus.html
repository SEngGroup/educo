ABOUT US

Educo was created in the year 2020 as part of a school based project. Educo is an online platform that aims at improving the learning experience for both tutors and learners.This community application aims to enhance collaboration between students and tutors enabling them to share knowledge and also enhance peer learning. Educo offers a different approach to learning providing a global reach of knowledge to its users at no cost.It aims at create an environment that is both friendly and educating where users are free to ask for help without being judged.
